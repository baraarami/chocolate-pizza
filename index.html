For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in asaucepan . Add brown sugar and stir to dissplve . Have all of the figs and toss in the saecepan with water and lemon juice . Cook over medium heat , stirring frequently , until you have a chunky-jammy mixture . Add salt with one or two stire , set aside and let cool completly.





Ice Cream: In small pot over medium heat , combine milk , and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barly lukewarm . Whisk in the egg yolk . Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.





Using an ice cream machine , pour liquid into the frozen basin according to manufacturer instruction . i,e.,let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone , fig jam mixture , and the nuts, continue to process for +/-10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into pyrex dish or glass tupperware . Freeze for at last two hours befor serving .
